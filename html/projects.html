Relevant projects I have worked on

Mine System
Topic: Software Architecture

IoT application to support the daily operation of a carbon mine. Focused on the control of the different environmental variables (temperature, light, humidity, carbon monoxide) that can affect the internal processes of each level of the mine.

Analytic models
Topic: Bussines Intelligence

To describe and characterize automobile accidents in Bogotá (Colombia). We wanted to identify factors that generate risk, as well as factors that magnify damage, with the purpose of creating strategies to reduce accident rate.

Wikipedia Views Analysis
Topic: Statistics

By means of a regression model, we had the objective of finding the most relevant variables that determine the number of times that the members of a fictitious university (created for the course) use Wikipedia.

Rest Tutorial
Topic: Transactional Systems

Basic tutorial to teach the implementation of REST services in the context of transactional systems. Includes descriptions of a 4-layer architecture and multiple tests to verify its operation.

SSE Tutorial
Topic: Security

Searchable symmetric encryption lab to explore the fundamentals of this concept and be able to make queries over encrypted information using a secure index.

Paseos Ecológicos
Topic: Transactional System

Transactional web application to manage ecological tours promoted by government organization. It includes the handling of inscriptions, calendars and activities.
